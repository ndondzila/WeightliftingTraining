No one does this alone

Though it's ultimately just you vs. the barbell, your journey in weightlifting depends on a circle of support, guidance, and solidarity. Some lifters train with bigger, serious teams. Others train alone in 'regular' gyms and utilize internet forums for help. I coach weightlifting in a structured yet flexible way that accomodates the various lifestyles of lifters.

Training Program

Custom plan geared towards your weaknesses, available equipment, and lifestyle.

Communication

Feedback on lifting videos for technical fixes, input on training notes and frequent general check-ins.

Event Coaching

Present at most national competitions; if a local meet is too far from St. Louis, I can usually connect you with someone.
