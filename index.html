No one does this alone

Though it's ultimately just you vs. the barbell, your journey in weightlifting depends on a circle of support, guidance, and solidarity. Some lifters train with bigger, serious teams. Others train alone in 'regular' gyms and utilize internet forums for help. I coach weightlifting in a structured yet flexible way that accomodates the various lifestyles of lifters. Please reach out if you're interested in having another person in your corner on your pursuit of bigger lifts!
